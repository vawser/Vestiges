#------------------------------------------------------------------
# Rings
#------------------------------------------------------------------
# Ring of Fortune
- Effect: Rare chance normal attacks become critical hits. 
- Source: Sold by Titchy Gren

# Butterfly Band
- Effect: jump higher and further, and reduces falling damage taken.
- Source: Sold by Cromwell the Pardoner

# Witch's Ring
- Effect: Every 2 minutes, restores 20% spell uses. 
- Source: Exchanged for the Old Iron King Soul.
